About Me

I am a PhD student in SFI Centre for Research Training in Machine learning Research Labs at University College Dublin where I am advised by Prof. Brian Mac Namee and Prof Patricia Maguire . My research is funded by Science Foundation Ireland(SFI). My CV is available here

I received an MSc in Data science from KTH Royal Institute of Technology and University of Nice Sophia Anitpolis through European Institute of Technology (EIT) digital master school scholarship.

Earlier, I graduated with a Bsc in Computer Science from National Chiao Tung University , through Taiwan Ministry of Foreign Affairs scholarship.

My research interests lie in the areas of Artificial intelligence, Applied Machine Learning and heathcare.

I am a Machine Learning lecturer at the Vitrual University of Ouagadougou since 2021. I am also a founder of AI-KING GROUP 2021, a AI research lab and consulting company. Earlier, I co-founded Yanfoma digital technomogy in 2016 and ExcelYaar
