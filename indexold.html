Algorithm

Machine learning model for odor molecules psychophysiology effects prediction.

I proposed a machine learning model to classify the odor molecules as an enhancing activity (or positive effect "+") or a decreasing activity (negative effect "-") for heartbeat rate variation and temperature. Such an approach will be important because it should allow us to design and optimize odorants or perfumes to affect our emotional state positively. My abstract has been accepted for two workshops at NeurIPS 2019 (WiML and Learning Meaningful Representations of Life). Presenting my work at two workshop poster sessions with a travel grant reaffirmed my passion for machine learning .
